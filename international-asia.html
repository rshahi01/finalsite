International Page

Asia

1. Japan
Japan is one of the most beautiful countries in Asia. It's capital, Tokyo, is home to amazing food, such as sushi, tempura, and ramen. The city's famous landmarks include the Tokyo Skytree and Sensō-ji. In the countryside, Mount Fuji is a must-see, treated as one of Japan's sacred mountains. For nature lovers, a hike up the mountain is filled with fresh air and beautiful scenery. In Kyoto, there a mix of both city-life and nature. Fushimi Inari-Tasina is one beautiful shrine to spot here, as well as the Philosopher's Walk, a canal lined with gorgeous cherry trees, and Arashiyama, a bamboo forest.

2. Thailand
Thailand is a must-visit for its delicious food and beautiful scenery. At its capital, Bangkok is a hub for authentic easy meals and a look at Thailand's culture. At the heart of the city is The Grand Palace, home to Thailand's royal family. For those who prefer vacationing on an island, Phuket is perfect. The small island features bright blue waters, white sand and tours around the island. Chiang Mai, a beautiful city in the countryside of Thailand, is on top of a mountain overlooking the country, with temples and monuments commemorating Buddha.

3. India
India is an experience you can enjoy. One of the most beautiful places to visit in India is Jaipur, part of the Golden Triangle Tourist Circuit and is known as “The Paris of India”. It's known for its jewelry stores galore, City Palace and pink buildings. Another beautiful place to visit is the Taj Mahal which is considered one of the 7 wonders of the world and it has a very interesting story as to how it was built! The Ajanta Caves is also a popular tourist attraction, and it includes 30 Buddhist cave monuments that date as far back as the 2nd century BC. Also, if you are vegetarian, India has many vegetarian options so there's something to eat for everyone!
