Domestic Page

West Coast &sightseeing

1. Seattle, Washington
If you are a lover of coffee, you would love the coffee culture in Seattle. There are also places to go whale watching as well as a beautiful city to be explored. Popular tourist attractions include the Museum of Flight, Woodland Park Zoo, a sky view observatory that allows you to see Seattle and neighboring cities and much more. Everyone can find something to do in Seattle.

2. Lake Tahoe, California
From snow in the mountains to crystal clear water, Lake Tahoe will give tourists plenty of beautiful scenery. If you enjoy nature, then you will enjoy Lake Tahoe and the variety of food it offers. South Lake Tahoe, where the tourists typically go, offers you sightseeing cruises, bike rides through the town and plenty of great hiking spots!

3. Napa Valley, California
If you love wine, then you will certainly enjoy your time at Napa Valley. Known for their wineries and vineyards, Napa Valley will also offer you spectacular views to go with it. Along with this is a wide variety of food, trails to go biking or a hike on, many music film and art events and an experience you will never forget.

4. Portland, Oregon
Portland is known for its indie food scene, hipsters and coffee shops. There is plenty you can do in this city such as bike riding around the city. You can also visit Multnomah Falls for nice scenery and a relaxing time. Also, the food trucks are famous in Portland as there is a wide variety of options. If you are a lover of books, there is a bookstore called Powells bookshop that will surely keep you entertained and impressed with the number of options they have.
