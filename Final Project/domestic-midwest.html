Domestic Page

Mid West Coast &sightseeing

1. Indianapolis
For cars fans, Indianapolis is a great place to visit. Take a trip to the home of the Indy 500, where the Indianapolis Motor Speedway is open for guests to visit the Hall of Fame of racing. The Indianapolis Motor Speedway Museum holds former Indy 500-winning cars, with exhibits showcasing automotive history. Anyone a fan of racing should be sure to visit Circle City.

2. Chicago
The largest city in the Midwest has the best art to offer in the US. Tour through the city's impressive architecture or a visit to the Art Institute of Chicago. Catch a show in one of Chicago's venues to witness the city's history of jazz. A trip to Chicago will be rich with culture and art.

3. Lake Superior
At Lake Superior, the largest freshwater lake in the world, there's a variety to do. Enjoy a ride down the Tahquamenon River and catch a glimpse of waterfalls. Or, visit the Devil's Kettle, a rock formation with an unexplainable missing river. Be surrounded by nature at Lake Superior.

4. Mount Rushmore
Mount Rushmore is one of the most prestigious national monuments in the US. Its mark on the Black Hills of South Dakota showcases US history and American labor. Those who enjoy the details of the sculpture, and the history of presidents and laborers should catch Mount Rushmore.
