1. New York City.
There are plenty of things to do such as eat good food, look at nice views, visiting the highline and central park. There are also world-famous museums such as MOMA if you are the type of traveler that enjoys art.

3. Miami, Florida.
Few places can compare to the white sand beaches of Miami. Along with this are a lot of high-end shopping areas, art buildings and boardwalks. If you are looking to relax during the day and party at night, Miami is the place to go.

4. Orlando, Florida.
Your children will love all the theme parks in Orlando while making you feel like a kid again. The theme parks are Walt Disney World and Universal Studios and each of them have unique attractions such as Seaworld, live shows and Harry Potter rides. There's something to do for everyone!
